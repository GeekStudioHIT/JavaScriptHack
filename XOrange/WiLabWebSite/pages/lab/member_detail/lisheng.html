李生教授简介

李生

教授，博导，语言技术研究中心主任。哈工大教育部-微软语言语音重点实验室主任,中文信息学会常务理事，国家自然科学基金评委，黑龙江省人才学会理事长，哈尔滨市专家咨询顾问委员会副主任，中共黑龙江省省委委员。主要研究方向：机器翻译、信息检索及自然语言处理。近年来完成国家自然科学基金、863计划等科研项目10余项，先后获部级科技进步二等奖4项、三等奖3项，在国内外学术刊物和会议上发表学术论文70余篇。

Prof. Li Sheng, Ph. D. superivisor, standing director of Chinese Information Processing Society, appraiser of National Science Foundation, Chairman of Executive Council of Heilongjiang Talents Society, vice-director of Harbin Expert Consultative & Advisory Committee, member of the Heilongjiang Council of CCP, director of HIT MOE-MS Key laboratory of NLP & Speech.

Research Interests: machine translation, information retrieval and natural language processing. In recent years, he has accomplished more than 10 projects funded by Natural Science Foundation of China or 863 Hi-tech Project. He is won 4 Second Prizes and 3 Third Prizes of the Ministry Science and Technology Progress Award. He has published more than 70 academic papers in the journals and conferences at home and abroad.
